Audience

The target audience for this website are small and big business owners, merchants, entrepeneurs and those interested on the local business of the Canton of Aargau.


Personas

Persona 1:
- 42 years old
- male
- married
- Father of 3 children
- local swiss farmer
- High school + Apprenticeship
- will access the website through a mobile device/ smartphone or a laptop


Persona 2:
- 50 years old
- male
- married
- Father of 2 children
- german owner of a big international company
- Phd in Internacional Business
- will access the website through a laptop or computer


Scenarios

- A foreign company that wants to expand its business to switzerland and would like to get informed on how and when to do it.


- A big company wanting to get counsel on the laws and taxations that are applicable to his/her company.


- A national citizen that wants to open his/her own business and wants to know what documents and forms does he/she need to have and hand in in the specific offices of the canton.


- A medium size business owner that wants to get informed of his rights and the benefits his company can get from the local and national governments (support).


- A local merchant that wants to open a small business and wants to get informed about the process and the helps he receives from the government by helping increase the market options in a small region.
