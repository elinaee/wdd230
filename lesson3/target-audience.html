Audience

The target audience for this website are small and big business owners, merchants, entrepeneurs and those interested on the local business of the Canton of Aargau.


Personas

Persona 1:
- 42 years old
- male
- married
- with 3 kids
- local swiss farmer


Persona 2:
- 50 years old
- male
- married
- with 2 kids
- german owner of a big international company


Scenarios

- A foreign company that wants to expand its business to switzerland and would like to get informed on how and when to do it, the laws, documents, rights and benefits that involve all that process.


- A local merchant that wants to open a small business and wants to get informed about the process and the helps he receives from the government by helping increase the market options in a small region.
